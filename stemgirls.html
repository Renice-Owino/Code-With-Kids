GIRLS IN STEM

Our Girls in STEM program is an initiative aimed at encouraging and promoting the participation of girls and young women in STEM fields. The program typically offers educational and hands-on learning opportunities designed to increase girls' interest and confidence in STEM and to provide them with the skills and resources needed to pursue STEM careers.

    Our program includes:
  • Workshops and hands-on projects.
  • Mentorship and networking opportunities.
  • STEM-related competitions and events.
  • Classroom and after-school programs.
The goal of our Girls in STEM program is to close the gender gap in STEM fields and to increase the representation of women in STEM careers. By providing girls with opportunities to develop their STEM skills and interests, these programs can play a role in creating a more diverse and inclusive STEM workforce.
