Our Focus Areas

Code With kids typically focuses on providing hands-on, project-based learning experiences in the fields of science, technology, engineering, and math.

Science

This is a way of learning about the world around us by asking questions and doing experiments to find answers.

Technology

This is the use of tools, machines, and other equipment to do things that make our lives easier or more interesting.

Engineering

This is the process of designing and building things like machines, buildings, and bridges. well.

Mathematics

This is a way of using numbers and symbols to solve problems and understand patterns.

About US

Code With Kids is a community-focused organization dedicated to providing affordable and accessible STEM education to children and young adults from low-income areas. Our goal is to empower the next generation of innovators by giving them the tools and resources they need to excel in the fields of Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics.

Overall, Code With Kids has had a positive and lasting impact on the kids who have participated and on the community as a whole. We are proud to have provided such a valuable resource and look forward to continuing to support the development of STEM education in the community.

Our Core Values

Core values are the fundamental beliefs and principles that guide the behavior and decisions of an individual or organization. At Code With Kids our core values include:

Collaboration:

We are a place where learners from different backgrounds and disciplines can come together to work on projects and share knowledge.

Inclusivity:

Code With Kids is welcoming and accessible to people of all race, backgrounds, and skill levels.

Creativity:

We encourage our learners to think creatively and explore new ideas, even if they might not be successful at first.

Innovation:

We foster an environment where learners can experiment and take risks in order to come up with new and innovative solutions to problems.

Learning:

Our learners learn new skills and concepts, whether through hands-on experimentation or more formal instruction.

Community:

Our learners are able to connect with others who share similar interests and build a sense of community.
