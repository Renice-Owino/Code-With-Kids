Our Focus Areas

Code With kids typically focuses on providing hands-on, project-based learning experiences in the fields of science, technology, engineering, and math.

Science

This is a way of learning about the world around us by asking questions and doing experiments to find answers.

Technology

This is the use of tools, machines, and other equipment to do things that make our lives easier or more interesting.

Engineering

This is the process of designing and building things like machines, buildings, and bridges. well.

Mathematics

This is a way of using numbers and symbols to solve problems and understand patterns.

Our Impact

Code With Kids has had a significant impact on the kids who have participated. Through their involvement, they have been able to learn about a range of STEM subjects and develop new skills. Many have noted that Code With Kids has sparked their curiosity and interest in these subjects, and we have seen tangible outcomes such as the completion of innovative projects.

Overall, Code With Kids has had a positive and lasting impact on the kids who have participated and on the community as a whole. We are proud to have provided such a valuable resource and look forward to continuing to support the development of STEM education in the community.
