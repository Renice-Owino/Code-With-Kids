DIJI MAMA

Diji Mama is a program aimed at empowering women mostly mothers with the digital skills they need to succeed in today's fast-paced, tech-driven world.
The program covers a wide range of topics, including :

  • Digital Literacy
  • The gig Economy
  • Digital Citizenship
  • Social Media Marketing
Through interactive workshops and hands-on training sessions, Diji Mama provides participants with a solid foundation in digital skills, including basic computer usage, internet navigation, and email management. The program also delves into the gig economy, helping the mothers understand how to leverage their skills and resources to earn income through online platforms and other flexible work arrangements.
Additionally, Diji Mama emphasizes the importance of digital citizenship and ethical behavior online, teaching mothers about privacy, security, and safe practices in the digital world. The program concludes with a comprehensive introduction to social media marketing, providing mothers with the tools and strategies they need to effectively promote their businesses and services online.
With a focus on empowering mothers, Diji Mama provides a supportive and inclusive environment where participants can gain the skills they need to succeed in today's digital landscape.
