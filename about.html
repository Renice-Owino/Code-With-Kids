Learn More About Our Work And Our Cultural Activities

Code With Kids has had a significant impact on the kids who have participated. Through their involvement, they have been able to learn about a range of STEM subjects and develop new skills. Many have noted that Code With Kids has sparked their curiosity and interest in these subjects, and we have seen tangible outcomes such as the completion of innovative projects.

Our maker space is equipped with a wide range of state-of-the-art equipment and technologies, including laptops, robotics kits, Science kits, and computer programming platforms. Our experienced and passionate educators lead hands-on sessions and workshops that encourage creativity, problem-solving and collaboration.

Our Programs

Mission

Our mission is to provide affordable and accessible STEM education to children and young adults from low-income areas, empowering them to become the next generation of innovators and leaders.

Vision

Our vision is to create a vibrant and inclusive community where every child has the opportunity to learn and grow through STEM, regardless of their background or financial circumstances. We believe that by fostering curiosity, creativity, and problem-solving skills in the next generation, we can help shape a brighter and more sustainable future for all.

SDG's We Focus On

Code With Kids plays a significant role in improving the lives of children and contributes to the achievement of several SDGs by providing learners with the resources, opportunities, and a solid foundation they need to learn, innovate, and become active and responsible citizens in the future.

Code With Kids provides learners from low-income areas with access to educational resources and opportunities that can help them lift themselves out of poverty.

Code With Kids fosters innovation and creativity that can help to develop sustainable solutions for food security and nutrition.

At Code With kids we provide learners with opportunities to learn about healthy living, such as through projects related to environmental health and food science.

Code With Kids provides learners with opportunities to learn new skills and concepts through hands-on experimentation and exploration.

We promote gender equality by providing equal opportunities for girls and boys to participate in our STEM activities and learn about STEM.

We provide our learners with opportunities to learn about clean energy technologies and how they can be used to reduce greenhouse gas emissions and protect the environment.

We offer access to educational resources and opportunities that can help them develop the skills and knowledge they need to secure decent work and contribute to economic growth.

We provide learners from disadvantaged backgrounds with access to educational resources and opportunities that may not be available to them otherwise, helping to reduce inequalities in STEM education.

We help to promote sustainable urban development by teaching our learners about environmental protection and resource conservation.

We provide learners with opportunities to learn about climate change and how they can take action to reduce greenhouse gas emissions and protect the planet.

We foster innovation and creativity that help to develop sustainable solutions through partnerships with local organizations and community groups to provide STEM resources to our learners.
